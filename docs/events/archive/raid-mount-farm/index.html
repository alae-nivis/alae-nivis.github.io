Raid Mount Farm

This event's difficulty is

EASY

It makes no difference even if you go AFK.

Rewards

See Raid Mount Catalogue.

Signing up

Signups are not needed for this event. Simply show up on time to participate.

Requirements to Join

For this event you do not need to unlock all. You can sit out the ones you have not unlocked.

Level 60 needed - Unlock the following duties under Duty Finder -> Raids 1 -> Savage Raids (Heavensward)

  • Alexander - The Burden of the Father (Savage)
  • Alexander - The Burden of the Son (Savage)
  • Alexander - The Soul of the Creator (Savage)

Level 70 needed - Unlock the following duties under Duty Finder -> Raids 1 -> Savage Raids (Stormblood)

  • Deltascape V4.0 (Savage)
  • Sigmascape V4.0 (Savage)
  • Alphascape V4.0 (Savage)

Level 80 needed - Unlock the following duties under Duty Finder -> Raids 1 -> Savage Raids (Shadowbringers)

  • Eden's Gate: Sepulture (Savage)
  • Eden's Verse: Refulgence (Savage)
  • Eden's Promise: Eternity (Savage)

For this event you do not need to unlock all. You can sit out the ones you have not unlocked.

There are also hard time requirements for this event. Make sure you are available and present from the start time until the end. Do not come late and do not leave early. Do not go off in the middle to eat for 30 minutes. There can be 5-minute coffee/toilet breaks but not much more than that.
