Bird/Doggo Farm

This event's difficulty is

EASY

It makes no difference even if you afk.

Get the bird/doggo you want! Everyone calls the bird/doggo they want and the party does those fights until everyone gets a bird/doggo!

(Don't roll on other people's mounts though, 1 per person!)

Rewards

See Bird Catalogue.

See Doggo Catalogue.

Requirements

Please make sure that you met the requirements before the start of the event. Please don't make people wait as you run around trying to unlock everything.

Level 70 required to unlock.

Unlock the following duties under Duty Finder -> Trials 1 -> High-end Trials (Heavensward)

  • The Limitless Blue (Extreme)
  • Thok ast Thok (Extreme)
  • The Minstrel's Ballad: Thordan's Reign
  • The Minstrel's Ballad: Nidhogg's Rage
  • Containment Bay S1T7 (Extreme)
  • Containment Bay P1T6 (Extreme)
  • Containment Bay Z1T9 (Extreme)

All of them are unlocked in Mor Dhona:

  • 2 of them are unlocked by talking to Alys
  • Remaining ones are given by Unukalhai in the Rising Stones

Unlock the following duties under Duty Finder -> Trials 1 -> High-end Trials (Stormblood)

  • The Pool of Tribute (Extreme)
  • Emanation (Extreme)
  • The Minstrel's Ballad: Shinryu's Domain
  • The Minstrel's Ballad: Tsykuyomi's Pain
  • The Jade Stoa (Extreme)
  • Hells' Kier (Extreme)
  • The Wreath of Snakes (Extreme)

The first four require completion of MSQ up to patch 4.3. The last three require full completion of Stormblood's side-MSQ (The Four Lords questline).

Make sure you unlock them before the event!
