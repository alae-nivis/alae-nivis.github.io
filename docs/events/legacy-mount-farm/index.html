Legacy Extremes and Savages

This event's difficulty is

EASY

It makes no difference even if you go AFK.

Get the mount you want! Everyone calls the mount they want and the party does those fights until everyone gets a mount!

Don't roll on other people's mounts though. 1 per person until everyone has 1. Then 2 per person until everyone has 2. And so on until the end of the event.

Rewards

See Doggo Catalogue.

See Dragon Catalogue.

See Raid Mount Catalogue.

Signing up

Signups are not needed for this event. Simply show up on time to participate.

Requirements to Join

For this event you do not need to unlock all. You can sit out the ones you have not unlocked.

ARR and Heavensward
