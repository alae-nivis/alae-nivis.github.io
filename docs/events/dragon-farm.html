Dragon Farm

Get the dragon you want! Everyone calls the dragon they want and the party does those fights until everyone gets a dragon*!

*Disclaimer: due to the fights' difficulty the event may run for many hours or some people might miss out on getting dragons.

(Don't roll on other people's birds though, 1 per person!)

SIGNING UP

Signups are mandatory for this event. Signup sheet can be found at the bottom of this page. Make sure you read all the requirements carefully and make sure you that you have met them.

If you entered multiple jobs, make sure you met the requirements for every job. If you don't, don't enter those jobs.

REQUIREMENTS TO JOIN

Level 80 required to unlock. Level 90 AND GEARED HIGHLY RECOMMENDED TO RUN.

Please make sure that you met the requirements before the start of the event. Please don't make people wait as you run around trying to unlock everything.

  • Item level 510 minimum
  • Item level 520 recommended
  • Pass The Cloud Deck (Extreme)'s Stone, Sky, Sea on every job you sign up with
  • Available for the full 3 hours (only toilet/coffee breaks)

Unlock the following duties

  • The Dancing Plague (Extreme)
  • The Crown of the Immaculate (Extreme)
  • The Minstrel's Ballad: Hades's Elegy
  • Cinder Drift (Extreme)
  • The Seat of Sacrifice (Extreme)
  • Castrum Marinum (Extreme)
  • The Cloud Deck (Extreme)

For every job that you listed on the signups, make sure that you have passed the Stone, Sky, Sea for The Cloud Deck (Extreme). i.e. if you signed up with 3 jobs, make sure you pass the Stone, Sky, Sea for The Cloud Deck (Extreme) with all 3 of those jobs.

The minimum item level is 510. If you have very little experience with endgame content, then ideally you should be at least 10 levels over the minimum requirements to not be completely useless.

There are also hard time requirements for this event. Make sure you are available and present from 19:00 GMT until 22:00 GMT. Do not come late and do not leave early. Do not go off in the middle to eat for 30 minutes. There can be 5-minute coffee/toilet breaks but not much more than that.

Also, watch a guide on the fights.

And of course, actually unlock the fights.
