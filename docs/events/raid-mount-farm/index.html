Raid Mount Farm

This event's difficulty is

EASY

It makes no difference even if you go AFK.

Get the raid mount you want! Everyone calls the raid mount they want and the party does those fights until everyone gets a raid mount!

Don't roll on other people's mounts though. 1 per person until everyone has 1. Then 2 per person until everyone has 2. And so on until the end of the event.

Rewards

See Raid Mount Catalogue.

Requirements

Please make sure that you met the requirements before the start of the event. Please don't make people wait as you run around trying to unlock everything.

Level 80 required to unlock.

Unlock the following duties under Duty Finder -> Raids 1 -> Savage Raids (Heavensward)

  • Alexander - The Burden of the Father (Savage)
  • Alexander - The Burden of the Son (Savage)
  • Alexander - The Soul of the Creator (Savage)

Unlock the following duties under Duty Finder -> Raids 1 -> Savage Raids (Stormblood)

  • Deltascape V4.0 (Savage)
  • Sigmascape V4.0 (Savage)

Unlock the following duties under Duty Finder -> Raids 1 -> Savage Raids (Shadowbringers)

  • Eden's Gate: Sepulture (Savage)

Make sure you unlock them before the event!
