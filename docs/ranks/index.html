Company Ranks

We believe that a free company is defined by its people and its community, so our promotion requirements reflects those values.

Recruit

Entry level rank. Welcome to the free company! Go ahead and introduce yourself to everyone. This rank acts as a trial period. Recruits that don't login for 180 days (ie, stopped playing) get kicked.

Soldier

Full member of the company. The only rank that requires you to progress a certain amount in the game.

Requirements:

  • Been in the company for two weeks
  • Unlocked mounts
  • Unlocked job (level 30)
  • Finished the A Realm Reborn (2.0) main story quests

Corporal, Sergeant

Established members of the company. NCOs.

Requirements:

Socialize, make friends, play with people, get to know other people in the company, make yourself be known in the company.

Lieutenant, Captain

Company officers, some power in the company.

May be demoted back to NCO if away for too long.

Requirements:

Take initiative, help people, contribute to company. Picked from sergeants as we need them.

First Commander, Lord Commander

Company leaders, unrestricted power.

Requirements:

Get to captain and wait for Yuki to retire.

Retired

People who's been away for more than a full patch. Soldiers and NCOs will be restored to original rank after coming back. Officers may be demoted back to NCO depending on time away.

Requirements:

Stop playing the game. Soldier rank or above. Recruits get kicked instead getting assigned this rank.
